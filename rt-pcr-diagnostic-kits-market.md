<p><p>RT-PCR diagnostic kits are essential for detecting RNA viruses, including SARS-CoV-2. The market is primarily segmented into two types: one-step and two-step RT-PCR kits. One-step kits combine reverse transcription and PCR amplification into a single reaction, simplifying the process and reducing contamination risk. In contrast, two-step kits separate these stages, allowing for greater flexibility and optimization of each step. Both types cater to different user needs in clinical and research settings, influencing their market demand and adoption.</p></p>
