<p><p>PEO software market can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise PEO software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering complete control and customization but requiring significant hardware and maintenance investments. In contrast, cloud-based PEO software is hosted on the vendor’s servers and accessed online, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. Cloud solutions often include automatic updates and easier integration with other tools, making them increasingly popular among businesses seeking efficiency and agility.</p></p>
