<p><p>The CNC ultrasonic welder market comprises two primary types: plastic and metal ultrasonic welders. Plastic ultrasonic welders utilize high-frequency vibrations to melt and fuse thermoplastic materials, ensuring strong, seamless joints in manufacturing processes such as packaging and automotive components. In contrast, metal ultrasonic welders employ similar ultrasonic technology to join metal parts, ideal for applications requiring precision and minimal thermal distortion, such as electronics and aerospace. Both types enhance efficiency and quality in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
