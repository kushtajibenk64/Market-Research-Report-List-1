In contrast, automatic knitting machines operate with minimal human involvement, enhancing productivity and consistency, ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Both types serve distinct market needs, with semi-automatic models offering flexibility and customization while automatic machines prioritize speed and volume, contributing significantly to industry advancements.</p></p>
