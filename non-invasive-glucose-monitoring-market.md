<p><p>The Non-Invasive Glucose Monitoring market includes two primary types: Wearable Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems and Table Top Blood Glucose Monitoring Systems. Wearable systems are designed for continuous monitoring, often utilizing sensors embedded in devices like smartwatches, providing real-time glucose readings without skin penetration. In contrast, Table Top systems require users to place their fingers or palm on a device, which utilizes optical sensors or other technologies to measure glucose levels painlessly. Both aim to enhance diabetes management and patient comfort.</p></p>
