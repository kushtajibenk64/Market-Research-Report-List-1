<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Home Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home ventilation systems market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Exhaust Ventilation Systems** remove stale indoor air, often using fans, creating a slight negative pressure that draws in fresh air through leaks.</p><p>2. **Supply Ventilation Systems** push filtered outdoor air into the home, controlling air quality while maintaining positive pressure.</p><p>3. **Balanced Ventilation Systems** feature both exhaust and supply mechanisms, ensuring equal air exchange.</p><p>4. **Energy Recovery Systems** transfer heat and moisture between outgoing and incoming air, enhancing energy efficiency and maintaining comfort.</p></p>
