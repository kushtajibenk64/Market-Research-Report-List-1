<p><strong>The Nuclear Power Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Globe Valve</li><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear power valve market includes various types essential for controlling fluid flow in nuclear plants. Globe valves provide precise flow regulation, making them suitable for controlling pressure. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities and are often used in high-pressure applications. Butterfly valves are lightweight and efficient, ideal for large pipe systems. Gate valves provide minimal flow resistance and are used for on/off control. Diaphragm valves are effective in handling corrosive and sanitary liquids, ensuring safety in nuclear operations.</p></p>
