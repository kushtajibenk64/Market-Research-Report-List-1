<p><p>The orthopedic regenerative surgical products market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Allografts** are grafts derived from human donors used to replace damaged tissues.</p><p>2. **Synthetic grafts** are man-made materials designed to mimic natural tissue functions.</p><p>3. **Cell-based products** involve the use of living cells to promote healing and tissue regeneration.</p><p>4. **Viscosupplements** are injectable substances that restore synovial fluid in joints, easing pain and improving mobility. Together, these products aid recovery and enhance orthopedic surgical outcomes.</p></p>
